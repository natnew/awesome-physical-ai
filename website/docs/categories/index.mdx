# Categories

The Awesome Physical AI list is organised into **14 canonical categories**. Each category mirrors the corresponding section of the root [`README.md`](https://github.com/natnew/awesome-physical-ai/blob/main/README.md) — the README remains the source of truth.

- [Simulators](./simulators.mdx) — Physics engines and high-fidelity simulation environments for robotics and embodied AI.
- [Datasets](./datasets.mdx) — Large-scale teleoperation, demonstration, and interaction datasets used to train robot policies.
- [Benchmarks](./benchmarks.mdx) — Task suites and standardised evaluations for manipulation, locomotion, and embodied reasoning.
- [Evaluation Methodology](./evaluation-methodology.mdx) — Harnesses, metrics, and methodology for measuring policy performance, robustness, and sim-to-real validity.
- [Robotics Foundation Models](./robotics-foundation-models.mdx) — Generalist policies and vision-language-action (VLA) models for robotic control.
- [World Models](./world-models.mdx) — Generative and predictive models of physical dynamics used for planning, simulation, and pretraining.
- [Manipulation](./manipulation.mdx) — Methods, models, and tools for grasping, dexterous manipulation, and contact-rich tasks.
- [Locomotion](./locomotion.mdx) — Legged, bipedal, and humanoid locomotion controllers, learning approaches, and reference platforms.
- [Sim-to-Real](./sim-to-real.mdx) — Techniques and case studies for transferring policies trained in simulation to real hardware.
- [Safety & Robustness](./safety-and-robustness.mdx) — Tools, benchmarks, and methodology for safe exploration, robustness testing, and failure-mode analysis.
- [Governance & Policy](./governance-and-policy.mdx) — Standards, frameworks, and policy guidance relevant to deploying Physical AI systems.
- [Production Patterns / Reference Architectures](./production-patterns.mdx) — Middleware, runtime stacks, and reference patterns for shipping robots in production.
- [Courses](./courses.mdx) — University courses and structured learning programs in robot learning and embodied AI.
- [Companies](./companies.mdx) — Organisations advancing Physical AI through foundation models, humanoids, and applied robotics.
